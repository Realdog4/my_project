Instant Car Shipping Quotes for Top-Rated Car Transport Service

Other car transport companies ship cars. We strive for more with our auto transportation services. Our low-priced car delivery service has changed the car shipping industry for the better. It's our life's work to provide travelers like you with auto shipping solutions that are fast, secure, and always affordable. If you're wondering how much it will cost you to ship a car with Nexus, our blazing-fast auto transport price calculator will shine some light on things. Your average auto transport cost per mile primarily depends on the distance of your vehicle shipment, and whether you ship a car with an open carrier or closed carrier.

Average Car Shipping Cost Per Mile

Less than 500 miles

Average cost

$630 - $930

We don't require any money down to book your order. You will only be charged once your auto transport is assigned to a driver and a pickup date is scheduled. We will contact you to let you know when your transport is dispatched.

Between 500-2500 miles

Average cost

$1,135 - $1.550

We require all of our car transport carriers to have their own active cargo insurance. We also carry our own contingent auto insurance policy, and a gap coverage option, so you are 100% covered with a $0 deductible.

Over 2500 miles

Average cost

$1,350 - $1,830

We understand that a delayed delivery can be a major inconvenience. That's why we offer to pay for your rental car if your delivery takes more than 14 days once picked up. We also pay for your car wash if your vehicle is delivered dirty.
